Affiliated Apps

Refrigedate

If you are constantly forgetting food you put in your fridge and throwing it away when it gets old, you can use Refrigedate to help you waste less food. Refrigedate can be used to keep track of how old the food in the fridge is and who it belongs to. Use this web app to reduce your food waste.

Overcooked

This web app allows users to share their extra food with other people in the area or charities. If you find yourself having lots of extra food but are unable to eat it all, you can use this web app to reduce your food waste while also helping other people in need.

Scavange

Do you have a lot of leftovers that you don't have a use for, and are thinking of throwing them away? Then you should use Scavange. This web app allows users to input food items and gets a recipe that uses those food items so that they are not wasted.

PreservIT

Not know how to preserve foods and extend their shelf life? Use PreservIT to find out. This web app contains information on how to preserve a variety of foods. Less spoiled food items means that you don't have to throw away as much food and you also have more time to use them.

iPicky

Buying food for other people that they don't like are allergic too? Use iPicky to create a list of your likes, dislikes and allergies. Combine lists with other people to create a master list that can be used by the person grocery shopping and cooking.
